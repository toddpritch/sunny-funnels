# What You Get

## Benefit 1: The First Transformation
Describe the first big win they get from your offer. Use specific numbers or outcomes.

Example: "Raise your prices by 50% without losing clients"

## Benefit 2: The Second Transformation
Describe the second benefit. Make it different from #1.

Example: "Land 3x more qualified leads from your content"

## Benefit 3: The Third Transformation
The final benefit. Think of this as the bigger-picture payoff.

Example: "Build a 6-figure business without burning out"

---

**Pro tip:** Each benefit should answer "What's in it for me?" from your audience's perspective. Not "what your course covers," but "what they'll be able to do after taking it."
