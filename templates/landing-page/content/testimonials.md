**How to get testimonials:**
1. Email past customers: "Can I get a quick testimonial?"
2. Record a Zoom call where they share results
3. Use Fathom to clip a 15-30 second segment from a call
4. Add to this file or embed the video

**Pro tip:** Video testimonials convert 2-3x better than text. Get at least one video testimonial before launching.
